Ometepe Island

Welcome to Ometepe Island

Ometepe Island is one of Nicaragua's most unique destinations. Formed by two volcanoes rising from Lake Nicaragua, the island offers a combination of adventure, nature, and culture unlike anywhere else in the world. Its lush landscapes, wildlife, and peaceful atmosphere make it a favorite destination for eco-tourists and outdoor enthusiasts.

Recognized as a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve, Ometepe is a paradise for travelers seeking natural beauty and unforgettable experiences.

Top Activities

  • Hike Concepción Volcano for incredible views and adventure.
  • Explore the trails and waterfalls of Maderas Volcano.
  • Swim in the crystal-clear waters of Ojo de Agua.
  • Visit local coffee farms and learn about traditional agriculture.
  • Discover ancient petroglyphs created by Indigenous cultures.
  • Kayak through rivers and wetlands filled with wildlife.
  • Enjoy cycling around the island's scenic roads.
