Granada

A Colonial Jewel of Nicaragua

Granada is one of the oldest colonial cities in the Americas and is famous for its colorful architecture, rich history, and vibrant cultural atmosphere. Located on the shores of Lake Nicaragua, Granada offers a perfect blend of history, nature, and adventure.

Top Activities

  • Explore the colorful colonial streets and plazas
  • Visit the historic Granada Cathedral
  • Take a boat tour through Las Isletas
  • Enjoy local restaurants and cultural events
  • Visit nearby Mombacho Volcano Nature Reserve
  • Shop at local artisan markets
