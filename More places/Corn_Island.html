Corn Island

Caribbean Paradise

Located off Nicaragua's Caribbean coast, Corn Island is a tropical paradise known for its crystal-clear waters, white-sand beaches, and laid-back island atmosphere. This destination offers a completely different side of Nicaragua, rich in Caribbean culture and natural beauty.

Top Activities

  • Snorkel among colorful coral reefs
  • Scuba dive in pristine Caribbean waters
  • Relax on beautiful white-sand beaches
  • Enjoy fresh seafood and local island cuisine
  • Explore the island by bicycle or golf cart
  • Take boat excursions to nearby cays
  • Watch unforgettable Caribbean sunsets
