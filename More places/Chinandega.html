Chinandega

Land of Volcanoes and Beaches

Located in northwestern Nicaragua, Chinandega is known for its fertile lands, volcanoes, beautiful Pacific beaches, and world-famous rum production. This region offers a unique combination of outdoor adventure, nature, coastal relaxation, and cultural experiences that showcase an important part of Nicaragua's heritage.

Top Activities

  • Visit Cosigüina Volcano and its crater lagoon
  • Relax on the beaches of Jiquilillo
  • Explore nature reserves and wildlife habitats
  • Enjoy surfing and water sports
  • Visit local agricultural communities
  • Experience fresh seafood and regional cuisine
  • Discover breathtaking coastal sunsets
  • Tour the Flor de Caña Rum Distillery and learn about Nicaragua's most famous rum
  • Explore the rum-making process from sugarcane cultivation to aging
  • Participate in rum tastings and discover award-winning Flor de Caña varieties

Featured Experience: Flor de Caña Tour

One of Chinandega's most popular attractions is the Flor de Caña experience. Visitors can tour the historic distillery, learn about more than a century of rum-making tradition, explore the aging warehouses, and enjoy guided tastings. This unique experience offers a glimpse into one of Nicaragua's most internationally recognized brands and an important part of the country's cultural heritage.
