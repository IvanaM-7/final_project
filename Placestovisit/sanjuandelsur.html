San Juan del Sur

Welcome to San Juan del Sur

Located on Nicaragua's Pacific coast, San Juan del Sur is one of the country's most popular beach destinations. Known for its stunning bay, vibrant nightlife, and laid-back atmosphere, this coastal town attracts surfers, adventure seekers, and travelers looking to relax by the ocean.

The town offers beautiful beaches, spectacular sunsets, and a variety of activities that make it the perfect destination for visitors of all ages.

Top Activities

  • Surf world-class waves at nearby beaches such as Playa Maderas.
  • Relax and enjoy the sun on beautiful sandy beaches.
  • Visit the famous Christ of Mercy statue for panoramic views of the bay.
  • Take a sunset sailing tour along the Pacific coast.
  • Explore local restaurants, cafes, and nightlife.
  • Experience sea turtle conservation projects during nesting season.
